Physics

What is physics?

Britannica defines physics as "is the branch of science that deals with the structure of matter and how the fundamental constituents of the universe interact. It studies objects ranging from the very small using quantum mechanics to the entires universe using general relativity"


Simply put, Physics studies how everything works together. They study gravity, motion, energy, force.
