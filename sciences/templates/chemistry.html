Chemistry

What is chemistry?

Chemistry is defined "as a branch of science that deals the identification of the substances of which matter is composed; the investigation of their properties and the ways in which they interact,combine, and change; and the these processes to form new substances.""


I like to think of chemistry as studying to learn about matter, what their special attributes allow them to do and why it works that way.
